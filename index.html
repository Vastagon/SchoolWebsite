Why I shouldn't go to college

Home You don't need a degree Plan

This is a project I designed in order to prove that not going to school and learning by myself may actually be a good idea. This may seem a bit impulsive, but I've been harboring these feelings since thinking about the bootcamp. The closer I get to real school the more anxious I get and the less I want to do it. The only reason I backed down on the bootcamp initially was because of fear, but I'm over that now.

I can't in good conscience, go to college. The more I think about it, the more I'm confident that I'm going to regret it.

I want to try this for a few main reasons:

  1. I don't want to give a bunch of people that don't care about my education money, just to get a piece of paper that can end with diminishing returns
  2. I want to prove to myself that I can take this slightly harder route. It'll prove to myself that I can do something outside of the norm. I've been in the norm and gone with the flow all of my life.

I learned the basics of HTML and CSS in order to make this website and show that I'm willing to learn on my own. I even enjoy learning like this. Schools and structured classes where I can't go at my own pace have always sucked. I started this website around the tenth and had no form of web development knowledge before it.
